# Component Selection

## NarrationEchoLayer

Use `NarrationEchoLayer` when:

- A talking-head segment needs one quiet idea marker.
- A key phrase from the narration should echo on the left side.
- The presenter remains the primary visual subject.
- There is no reliable source asset for a factual visual.

Do not use it:

- As a chapter title system.
- For every sentence.
- In the subtitle safe zone or presenter safe zone.

Recommended frequency: only when the segment needs a light visual anchor.

## Episode-Specific Effects

Use an episode-specific effect when:

- The approved storyboard needs a one-off visual treatment.
- The effect is not expected to be reused at least twice.
- The visual should stay local to the episode.

Do not promote one-off effects into the public component library.
